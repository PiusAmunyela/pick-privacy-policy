Privacy Policy for PICK

Introduction

Welcome to PICK ("we", "our", or "us"). We recognize and respect the importance of protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in connection with our mobile application, PICK ("the App" or "Platform").

PICK is a Namibian Business-To-Business-To-Customer (B2B2C) platform designed to sell goods to retailers. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to handling your personal data with transparency, care, and in accordance with international standards, including those required by the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

a. Personal Identification Information:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth (if provided)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Account login details

Purpose: To verify your identity, allow account registration, enable secure login, and provide customer support.

b. Shipping Information:

  • Delivery Address
  • City / Region

Purpose: This data is used solely for order fulfillment and is shared only with verified local courier services to ensure delivery.

c. Device & Usage Data:

  • IP address
  • Device type and OS
  • App interaction data (clicks, screens visited)
  • Error logs and crash reports

Purpose: To help us optimize app performance, detect issues, and improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your PICK account
  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To provide efficient customer service and support
  • To communicate with you regarding purchases, updates, or support issues
  • To enhance the functionality, performance, and security of our platform
  • To comply with applicable laws, legal obligations, and resolve disputes
